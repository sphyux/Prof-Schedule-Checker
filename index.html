About CDM Instructor's Schedule Checker

Welcome to the Instructor Schedule Checker, a tool designed to simplify the lives of students by providing easy access to their instructors' schedules throughout the week.

Our goal is to enhance communication and facilitate efficient interaction between students and instructors. Explore a comprehensive view of your instructor's schedule for the entire week. Easily find the schedule for a specific instructor or course using our intuitive filtering and search options.


Our mission is to empower students by providing a user-friendly and efficient platform for accessing and understanding their instructors' schedules. We aim to enhance communication and collaboration between students and instructors, ultimately contributing to a more streamlined and productive academic experience.
